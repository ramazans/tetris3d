Usage

Drop in the markup, set two variables. That’s the whole API.

--cube-colorBase hue. Tints & shades are auto-derived.
--cube-sizeAny length. Radius, bevel & shadow scale with it.
<div class="cube" style="--cube-color:#57c47a; --cube-size:120px"></div>
Pure CSS · matte finish · soft bevel · pillow depth · configurable via two custom properties
